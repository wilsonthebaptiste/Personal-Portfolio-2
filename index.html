About Me

Full Stack Engineer

Hello everyone! I'm Wilson Jean-Baptiste, a full stack developer with a rich background in both chemical engineering and technology. What enticed me to make the transition from chemical engineering to technology are the limitless possibilities it offers for building and innovation. Having readily available tools to create solutions that will significantly improve people's lives motivates me every day to grow as a full stack engineer. My chemical engineering background has equipped me with a strong analytical mindset, making me an effective problem solver and a quick learner. Currently, I am focused on expanding my portfolio, which includes this website and several other projects that showcase my capability to transform ideas into reality.

One fundamental belief I hold is that all lie experiences connect to one another and so it is important to be well rounded. Outside of coding, I have a passion for reading comics, staying active through health and fitness, and expressing my creativity through drawing. These activities not only keep me balanced but also fuel my creativity and drive within the tech world. I am eager to bring my skills and passion for development to a dynamic team where I can contribute to meaningful projects and continue growing as a developer.
